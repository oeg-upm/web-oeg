José Ángel Ramos Gargantilla is a member of Ontological Engineering Group (OEG) at UPM. He graduated in Computer Science from UPM (2001) and , currently, is a PhD student at UPM in Artificial Inteligent Department.

His research is in Merging and Mapping in the field of Ontologies.

He'd participated in the organization on differents international events: EKAW02 and TKE2012 Conferences and the SSSW03, SSSW04, SSSW05, SSSW06, SSSW07, SSSW08, SSSW09, SSSW11, SSSW12 and SSSW13 summerschools, and he participates in the OEG Managing.

Projects

In the context of his research lines, nowadays he has participated in the following research projects:

  • OntoWeb (IST-2000-29243)
  • Knowledge Web (FP6-507482)
  • Infraestructura tecnológica de servicios semánticos para la web semántica (TIN-2004-02660)
  • NeOn (FP6-027595)
  • GeoBuddies (TSI 2007-65677)
  • España Virtual (CENIT2008-1030)
