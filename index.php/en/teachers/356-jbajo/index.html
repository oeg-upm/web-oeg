Javier Bajo is Associate Professor at the ETS Ingenieros Informáticos - Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (UPM). He is currently Coordinator of the Research Master in Artificial Intelligence at UPM. Previously, he hold the position of Associate Professor at the Universidad Pontificia de Salamanca (2003 to 2012) and Director of the Data Center in the same University (2010-2012).

He obtained a PhD in computer sciences from the Universidad de Salamanca (with honours) (2007) and a Master in E-Commerce in the same University (2006). He obtained a bachelor in computer sciences at the Universidad de Valladolid (2001) and a MsC in computer sciences at the Universidad Pontificia de Salamanca (2003).

His research efforts are focused on multi-agent systems, social computing and ambient intelligence. He has participated in more than 50 research projects (funded by European Commission, National or Regional entities) and contracts with private companies, acting as principal researcher in 11 of these projects.

He is co-author of more than 300 scientific publications (books, Journal papers, conference papers). 49 of these publications have been published in international journals indexed in the ISI JCR reference index. He has been co-chairman of the organizing committee of more than 30 recognized international conferences (ACM SAC, IEEE FUSION, PAAMS, ...).
