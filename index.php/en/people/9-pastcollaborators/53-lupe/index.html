Dr. Guadalupe Aguado de Cea is Associate Professor at the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (UPM). She holds a MSc in Translation by Universidad Complutense de Madrid (UCM) and a Ph.D in English Philology (1992) by UCM.

She has been the coordinator of the Doctorate Programme in Applied Linguistics at the Department of Applied Linguistics to Science and Technology (UPM) since 2001. She has been collaborating in the Ontology Engineering Group since 1996.

Her current research activities include, among others: Terminology and Translation, Sentiment analysis and Linguistic Engineering, specially modelling linguistic knowledge in ontologies in its application to the Semantic Web. Among others, she has participated in LIDER, MOnnet, NeOn, CONTENTWEB, (Technological Platform for the Semantic Web: Ontologies, Natural language Analysis and e-commerce); AMUSEMENT; Ontogeneration: Query System for information retrieval by generating texts in the Chemicals field; POINTER Proposals for Operational Infrastructure for Terminology in Europe.

She has published many papers in these fields and she is author of the Diccionario comentado de terminología informática 1994/1996 Madrid: Paraninfo. She has also lectured in different universities as a visiting scholar. She was program chair of TKE 2012 and TIA 2013. She is also the convenor of the AENOR CTN_191 Terminology Committee, the corresponding Spanish Committee of ISO TC 37.
