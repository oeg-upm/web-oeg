Victor Saquicela Galarza  is a PhD student in Artificial Intelligence at the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid. He has been a member of the  Ontology Engineering Group since 2008 in the Artificial Intelligence Department of the Faculty of Informatics. He is an Engineer in Informatics (2002)  from Universidad de Cuenca in Ecuador  and Master in Software Engineering (2004) from Universidad Politecnica de Madrid in Spain.

Victor has previously worked as programmer analyst and network administrator.

His research activities are focused on Semantic Web, Data Integration, Geospatial Semantic Web and Linked Data.

Projects

In the context of his research lines, nowadays he is participating in the following research projects:

  • GeoBuddies. Anotación semántica colaborativa con dispositivos móviles en el Camino de Santiago (TSI2007-65677-C02-01).
  • España Virtual (ALT0317).
