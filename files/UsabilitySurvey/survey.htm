The survey to asses the usability of the collaborative ontology editiring framework are based on the SUMI standard. SUMI (Software Usability Measurement Inventory) is a rigorously tested and proven method of measuring software quality from the end user's point of view and it is mentioned in the ISO 9241 standard as a recognised method of testing user satisfaction.
